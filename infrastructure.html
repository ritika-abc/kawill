Modern & Innovative Manufacturing Unit

Our offered components are manufactured by making use of optimum quality raw materials and advanced machines at our well-equipped infrastructure unit. With the support of our ultra-modern and the latest infrastructural facility, we have attained a huger client base. We also have further segregated our infrastructure facility into different units like manufacturing unit, administration unit, quality testing unit, warehousing & packaging unit, etc.

All these integrated units are well-resourced with all the required tools, machines and technology in order to have flawless and well-defined business operations. In order to maintain timeliness and in order to enhance the productivity, the installed machines are updated on regular basis.

Keshav Brass Industries also have a separate R & D unit for continuous research and development to come out regularly with new products and improve the quality of existing products. Refining and revising of processes is a major strength of our research team.
