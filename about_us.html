About Company

Manufacturer of Machine Screws, Metal Fasteners & Precision Turned Components

Incepted in the year 1990, in Jamnagar (Gujarat, INDIA) we “Keshav Brass Industries” are a Sole Proprietorship firm recognized as the foremost manufacturer and exporter of a broad assortment of Machine Screw, Cheese Head Screw, Self Tapping Screw, SEMS Screw, Two Start Head Screw, Trilobular Screw etc. The products provided by us are highly treasured for their remarkable features like easy to install, precise dimensions, rust resistance, fine finish, robust construction, application specific design, etc. Our offered components are manufactured by making use of optimum quality raw materials and advanced machines at our well-equipped infrastructure unit.

The components offered by us are well-examined on variegated parameters of quality in order to assure their flawlessness. The professionals who are associated with us help us to meet the numerous needs and necessities of the patrons.
